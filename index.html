How It Works

Our simple 3-step process makes civic issue reporting effortless and effective

1

Capture the Issue

Take a photo of the problem (pothole, broken streetlight, etc.) and our app automatically tags the GPS location.

2

AI Analysis

Our system analyzes the report using computer vision and NLP to assess severity and categorize the issue.

3

Resolution

The report is routed to the appropriate department with priority based on severity, and you can track progress.

User Journey

From reporting to resolution - see how our system works for citizens and authorities

1. Report Submission

Citizen takes a photo of the issue, adds a brief description, and submits through the app.

2. AI Processing

Our system automatically categorizes the issue, assesses severity, and assigns priority.

3. Department Routing

The report is sent to the appropriate municipal department based on issue type and location.

4. Resolution Workflow

Municipal workers receive the report, address the issue, and update the status.

5. Citizen Notification

The citizen receives updates and a final resolution notification with before/after photos.
